### Lifetime Value (LTV):

**Team Plan:**
- ARPU: $99/month
- Average lifespan: 24 months (year 1), 36 months (year 2+)
- **LTV = $99 √ó 24 = $2,376 (year 1)**
- **LTV = $99 √ó 36 = $3,564 (year 2+)**

**Business Plan:**
- ARPU: $299/month
- Average lifespan: 36 months
- Expansion revenue: +20% (add-ons, upsells)
- **LTV = $299 √ó 36 √ó 1.2 = $12,916**

**Enterprise Plan:**
- ARPU: $1,330/month (average)
- Average lifespan: 48 months
- Expansion revenue: +30%
- **LTV = $1,330 √ó 48 √ó 1.3 = $82,992**

- Team (60%): $3,564 √ó 0.6 = $2,138
- Business (30%): $12,916 √ó 0.3 = $3,875
- Enterprise (10%): $82,992 √ó 0.1 = $8,299
- **Total Blended LTV = $14,312**
