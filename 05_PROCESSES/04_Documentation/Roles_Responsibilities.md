# 04_Documentation - Roles & Responsibilities

→ [Overview](./Overview.md) | [Steps](./Process_Steps.md) | [Data](./Data_IO.md) | [Test](./Test_Scenario.md)

---

## ACCOUNT [Lead, R]

**Действия:**
- Create orders from media plan
- Coordinate with Legal/Finance
- Send docs to client
- Track signing/payment

**Time:** 5-7ч

---

## PM [Controller, A]

**Действия:**
- Monitor progress
- Check completeness
- Escalate delays

**Time:** 2-3ч

---

## LEGAL [Contracts, R]

**Действия:**
- Prepare contract templates
- Review terms
- Internal approval

**Time:** 3-5ч

---

## FINANCE [Invoices, R]

**Действия:**
- Create invoices
- Set payment terms
- Confirm payment received

**Time:** 2-3ч

---

## CLIENT [Approver, A]

**Действия:**
- Review contracts
- Sign
- Pay invoices

**Time:** 2-4ч
